History & Temperament

The Vietnamese Ridgeback, native to Phu Quoc Island, is one of only three ridgeback breeds in the world. Originally bred for hunting and guarding, they remain a primitive and highly intelligent breed.

Breed Characteristics

  • The Ridge: A line of hair growing in the opposite direction along the spine.
  • Agility: Webbed toes make them excellent swimmers and agile climbers.
  • Temperament: Fiercely loyal, protective, yet incredibly affectionate with their families.
