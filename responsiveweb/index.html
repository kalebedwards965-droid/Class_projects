About the Car

The 2023 Toyota GR86 is an amazing sports car. It has great handling and is super fun to drive around town.

The story of the GR86 starts way back in the 1980s with the legendary Toyota AE86 Corolla. It wasn't the fastest car on the road, but its lightweight, rear-wheel-drive setup made it incredibly fun to drive. It became famous in the Japanese street racing and drifting scenes, and even got a massive pop-culture boost from anime. That classic formula of a lightweight, perfectly balanced, and affordable sports car is the DNA that Toyota wanted to keep alive.

Fast forward to today, and the 2023 GR86 is the ultimate modern version of that classic recipe. Toyota teamed up with Subaru to build a chassis that handles like a dream and dropped in a punchy 2.4-liter boxer engine. It keeps everything that made the original 86 great—it's rear-wheel drive, focuses on driver connection, and is built purely for the joy of the road, whether you are carving through tight mountain corners or just cruising around town.
